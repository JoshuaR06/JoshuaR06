Lighthouse is an organization and reflection app for people managing dissociative identity disorder (DID) or similarly complex internal organization needs. It is designed to help different parts of a person’s inner experience track important details in one steady, readable space.

What Lighthouse helps organize

Lighthouse is built for practical day-to-day use. It focuses on helping you keep key information together, with less friction and more consistency.

Journals and reflections

Capture entries, context, and notes in one place with a calm, readable layout.

Mood and check-ins

Track mood and related patterns over time to support personal self-management.

Calendar and routines

Keep schedules, reminders, and recurring structure visible and easy to review.

Medication notes

Store medication-related details as personal records for organization purposes.

Privacy overview

Lighthouse is designed to be local-first. The app does not require a developer-hosted account system, and most information stays on your device unless you choose to share specific categories.

Local-first by default

Content you enter is stored on your device by default.

No required account

You can use Lighthouse without creating an account with the developer.

Optional CloudKit sharing

If you choose to share selected categories with friends, sharing uses Apple CloudKit and a sharing link.

Shared category updates

When shared categories are updated, those shared updates may sync through the user’s iCloud/CloudKit environment.

Shared categories may include mood, calendar, journals, and medication, depending on the features you choose to use.
