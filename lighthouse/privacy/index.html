Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026

Lighthouse ("the App") is developed and operated by Joshua Reid ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Content you create in Lighthouse is primarily stored on your device unless you enable sync or sharing features.

  • On-device data: Information you add to the App is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad by default.
  • CloudKit and iCloud data: If you enable iCloud sync or sharing features, selected App data may be stored in your iCloud account using Apple CloudKit.
  • No separate Lighthouse account: We do not require you to create a separate developer-hosted account to use the App.
  • Diagnostics and logs: We do not currently run third-party advertising trackers in the App. Apple may still process limited technical data as part of iOS, iCloud, CloudKit, or system-level diagnostics.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to provide core App functionality, including local storage, optional syncing, and optional collaboration or sharing features you choose to use.

  • To save and display your content in the App.
  • To sync your content across your own Apple devices when iCloud sync is enabled.
  • To support user-initiated sharing features through CloudKit.

3. Sharing

The App may allow you to share selected information with other people through Apple and CloudKit-based sharing tools. Shared data is limited to what you intentionally choose to share through the App.

We do not sell your personal information. We also do not share your App content for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage

By default, your content is stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud/CloudKit features, Apple may store and process relevant data on your behalf through your iCloud account to provide sync, backup, and sharing functionality.

Because CloudKit relies on Apple services, Apple may handle data according to Apple's own terms, conditions, and privacy practices. You can manage iCloud settings from your Apple device settings.

5. Payments and Monetization

The App does not currently require payments to use core functionality unless explicitly stated in-App. In the future, we may introduce optional paid features, subscriptions, donations, tips, or other monetization tools.

If monetization features are added, payment-related data may be processed by Apple (for example, through in-app purchases) and/or other third-party payment processors. We do not intend to store full payment card details directly unless we explicitly add that capability and update this policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Lighthouse is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can review and address the situation.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy over time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or new features. When we make updates, we will revise the effective date shown on this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: josh@jink.io
