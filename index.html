What Lighthouse-DID Hub is built to do

The app is designed as a personal organization and self-management space. It is meant to help users keep important information together in a more structured and accessible way.

Private journaling

Write and review personal entries in a space that remains on your own device.

Daily organization

Keep routines, reminders, and personal notes in one place with a simple interface.

Clear design

A calm visual layout helps reduce friction and makes the app easier to navigate over time.

Privacy by design

Privacy is a core part of Lighthouse-DID Hub. The app is structured to reduce unnecessary data exposure and keep control with the user.

Local-first storage

Information entered into the app is stored on the user’s device rather than on external servers.

No tracking

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or location tracking.

No account required

Users do not need to create an account or provide identifying information to use the app.

User-controlled sharing

If a sharing feature is added in the future, only content the user explicitly chooses to share would be sent.

Lighthouse-DID Hub is intended as a private organizational tool, not a data-collection platform.

Important note

Lighthouse-DID Hub is a supportive self-management app. It is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, therapy service, or emergency resource.

If you or someone else may be in immediate danger, contact emergency services right away or reach out to a qualified crisis resource in your area.
