Privacy Policy

Lighthouse-DID Hub (“the App”) is developed and operated by Joshua Reid (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

  • The App does not require account creation.
  • The App does not collect names, email addresses, or identifiers.
  • The App does not track location.
  • The App does not use analytics or advertising trackers.
  • The App does not store user entries on external servers.

All information entered into the App, including journal entries, schedules, or system-related notes, is stored locally on the user’s device only.

2. How Information Is Used

Because we do not collect user data, we do not sell, rent, share, analyze, or monetize that data. Information entered into the App is used only for the App’s functionality on the user’s own device.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data remains under the user’s control on their device. We do not have access to this data and cannot retrieve, recover, or delete it on the user’s behalf.

4. Optional Sharing Features

If the App includes optional features that allow a user to share selected information with another person, sharing occurs only when the user intentionally initiates it. We do not store or retain shared content.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

Lighthouse-DID Hub is intended for adults only and is not directed to children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will appear on this page with a revised effective date.
